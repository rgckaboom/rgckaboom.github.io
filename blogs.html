Project Borter Begins

June 19, 2020 by Admin

The project Borket has finally begun.

Its has been quite sometime since I began planning this project but didn't start because I didnt had the required skills, I still don't have the required skills.

Never the less, I have started because as I was thinking what I would like to do for the cs50W project0, the one thing which came to my mind was this.

I always wanted to work on a project which is meaningful and will facilitate other as well.

How did it start?

I picked up the habit of reading books about 2-3 years ago and soon found myself short on money to buy paperbacks even though now I own a kindle but then I realized wont it be great if people could exchange their books

Why NERIST?

Because its the place where I study and so why not start small and if people like it, I surely do plan to expand it.

I have been in NERIST for four years now and the one thing which I think it lacks is a place or an organisation in which like minded students can meet and socialise. There's no book club , dance club or club of any sort in our institute and so the students don't get the opportunity to collaborate since they don't know others who are like minded.

To bring a change

This platform is intended to change it. I have chosen books because its a niche which I like and thought that its a good place to start.

The key objectives of this platform will be to:

  • Connect like minded individuals.
  • Facilitate exchange of books.
  • Eventually expand to other fields of interest.
